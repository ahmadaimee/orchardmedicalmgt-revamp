Our RCM Process

1

Charge Capture & Coding

We verify and validate all charges against documentation, applying accurate CPT and ICD-10 codes to minimize denials before submission.

2

Clean Claims Submission

Claims are scrubbed against payer-specific rules and submitted electronically within 24 hours of service.

3

Payment Posting & Reconciliation

AI-powered automated payment posting reconciles EOBs and ERAs in real time — eliminating manual errors.

4

Denial Management

Every denial is tracked, categorized, and worked systematically — with root-cause analysis to prevent recurrence.

5

Reporting & Insights

Monthly AI-generated reports give you full visibility into your revenue performance with actionable recommendations.
