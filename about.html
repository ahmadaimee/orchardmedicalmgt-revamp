Our Mission

We Exist to Protect Your Practice’s Financial Future

At Orchard Medical Management, our mission is simple: fortify private practices by focusing relentlessly on your financial health so that you — and your clinical team — can focus on patient care.

We founded OMM in 2004 with a conviction that independent practices deserve the same quality of administrative support as large health systems — without the bureaucracy. Over twenty years later, that conviction has never wavered. If anything, it has sharpened. Today, we combine deep healthcare expertise with AI-powered automation to deliver results that weren’t possible even five years ago.

We measure our success by one thing: the financial and operational health of the practices we serve. When your revenue grows, your denials shrink, and your team stops drowning in administrative work — that’s why we come to work every day.
