AI Solutions

Six AI-Powered Solutions, One Unified Practice

Every automation OMM deploys is purpose-built for healthcare — HIPAA-aware, EMR-integrated, and designed to improve both financial outcomes and patient experience.

AI Calling Agent

Our AI calling agent conducts natural, human-like voice conversations with patients for appointment reminders, recall outreach, collections follow-ups, satisfaction surveys, and more — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Dramatically reduces no-shows, recovers overdue balances, and frees your front desk to focus on in-person patient experience.

Live & Active

HL7 & FHIR Integration Services

We design, build, and maintain HL7 and FHIR data exchange pipelines between your EMR, payers, labs, imaging centers, and ancillary systems. From ADT and SIU messages to FHIR APIs, our integration layer keeps clinical and financial data synchronized and actionable across every touchpoint in your care ecosystem.

Deployed

Automated Patient Scheduling

AI-powered scheduling intelligently fills appointment slots based on provider availability, patient history, appointment type, and demand patterns. Patients can self-schedule online, by phone, or via SMS. The system automatically handles reschedules, waitlists, and same-day cancellation backfill — maximizing your daily patient volume.

Deployed

Automated Billing & Payment Posting

End-to-end automated patient billing combined with intelligent payment posting that reconciles EOBs, ERAs, and patient payments in real time. Our system identifies posting errors, flags underpayments, and automatically routes exceptions for human review — dramatically reducing AR days and administrative overhead.

Deployed

AI Coding Assistance

Our AI coding engine cross-references clinical documentation against CPT and ICD-10 code sets in real time, flagging under-coded or over-coded encounters before submission. This dramatically reduces denials from coding errors and ensures your practice captures every dollar of reimbursement it’s entitled to.

Deployed

Monthly AI Practice Insights

Every month, your practice receives a fully automated AI-generated report covering revenue trends, denial patterns, payer mix, scheduling efficiency, cash flow projections, and provider-level productivity scores — with plain-English recommendations you can act on immediately.
